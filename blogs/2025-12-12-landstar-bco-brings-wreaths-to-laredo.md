Laredo, Texas, along with over 4,600 other cemeteries across the county, commemorates its fallen veterans with a Wreaths Across America remembrance ceremony and wreath-laying event. Now in its fourth year, the city has BCO Bob Mead and his wife to thank for bringing the remembrance event to Laredo.
